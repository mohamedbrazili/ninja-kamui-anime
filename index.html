What's the Story?

NINJA KAMUI follows Joe Logan (voiced by Jeremy Gee), a former ninja living in hiding in rural America with his family to escape his past. But when his old clan attacks, seeking vengeance for his leaving they murder his family. Joe survives the attack and vows revenge against those who took his loved ones from him. Using his honed ninja skills, Joe must face off against the cybernetically enhanced assassins who are hunting him down.
